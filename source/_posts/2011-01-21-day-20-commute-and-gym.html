Finally, I can use the gym, I'm paying for!  After signing up and paying on MondayI was told I had to do an induction before being allowed to use the gym.  Well, I went last night and after being wieghed, measured; height, blood pressure and pulse: 14st 10lb, 193cm, 83/130, 48 bpm.  I told them I had used the gym before and after being tested I could use a machine, they said I was free to use the gym! So I spent 30 mins doing some weights and finished off with a 5 minute row.

If anyone has any good advice on resources for planning a core strength workout - let me know!  Its my main area of focus for the gym in prep for my first Olympic triathlon in July!

The commute was good, did my fastest time to kings cross in the evening!  However, I did see on cyclist take out a young lady who stepped out into the cycling lane at a traffic lights without looking - note to self cycle defensively!
