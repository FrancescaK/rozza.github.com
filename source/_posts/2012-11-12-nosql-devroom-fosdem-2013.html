Awesome news after a hiatus of 3 years theres going to be a NoSql devroom at fosdem in february.  If you don't know fosdem - its a great conference based in Brussels covering all things open source, theres no registration, just turn up with the other 5,000 free and open source fans.

So if your interested in NoSql or big data then propose a talk!  We're looking for 25 minute or 50 minute talks - sign up here: bit.ly/nosql-devroom-2013 alternatively sign up to the mailing list and help make the devroom great!
