Day 5 - Dark and Drizzle

Jan 5th, 2011

I was planning on doing a slightly longer run tonight, but forgot I was needed to look after the barins.  So I opted for a loop into town and round.  Its a good run with a fast downhill straight in and then a long gentle up slope back and round.

It was all going well, then runkeeper annouced I was doing 13min miles - which when I thought I was doing a fine pace seemed very odd. Distracted I wondered across a road only to notice a car was also planning on using it. So I stopped and waved an apology! So I took the morale there to be; stop being obsessed by stats - it’ll only ruin a perfectly good run!

I’m planning on joining a gym tomorrow, so I’ll be able to start so cross training in and reduce the wear on my legs.
