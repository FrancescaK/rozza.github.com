Janathon Done!

Jan 31st, 2011

Wow!  I’ve done it, I exercised every day in January! I started with a swim so I thought I’d end with a swim - not in the north sea but a few lengths of the swimming pool. I’m pleased with myself and I think it has set up a base level of activity that I can extend and improve over the coming months as I train for my triathlon in July.

I really enjoyed the atmosphere; the tweets and the posts about janathon. The community made it easier and it was good to see that others encouraged people on and offered advice as well - great work all!

Day 30 - Run, Gym, Run

Jan 30th, 2011

Back to the gym for a core strength workout. Its amazing that in just 4 sessions my strength at some of the machines seems to have improved markedly.  Probably more to do with getting use to dealing with lactic acid build up when doing weights, rather than an increase of strength but I’ll take any rewards as they come!

The best bit about today was I ran to the Gym did a work out and then ran back - a real win as doing cardo work in the gym isn’t as fun and psychologically the gym has got a whole load closer to home!

Weights: 35 mins
Cardo: 20 mins

Day 29 - a Run Out

Jan 30th, 2011

A crisp Saturday afternoon led to a great 30 minute run, on a route I first did last summer - when I was first starting out with running, I picked a half marathon as my target and wanted to include a bit of a hill and cross country to keep the training varied.  This short run soon became a favourite before work run but had long been abandoned due to not enough light.

A good blast round, my right knee was still giving me some gyp but not as bad as last week.  So I finished off by doing a long session of stretching afterwards. I’m hoping for more answers on Monday when I’m off to see the doc.
