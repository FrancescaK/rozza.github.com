Day 7 - Week One Done!

Jan 7th, 2011

Good run with Pete we volunteered to come out and support me whilst doing Janathon and as a keen cyclist he was slightly anxious about running and pace; well no need he set a good pace and I had to slow him down as I was suffering!

Day 6 - Fartlek Fun!

Jan 6th, 2011

Today my daughter started school she was extra excited, I was excited and anxious and the result was a bad nights sleep for all!  She loved it and I had a school dinner with her to help introduce the canteen - school dinners have improved, curry and crumble!  Lovely!

I also found a gym its quite small but has the kit I need is local, relatively cheap and also gives free access to the local swimming pool - so win!  The downside, is I have to wait until the 17th to join so I can get Nov and Dec free (if I’m still a member).  I’m booked in then and can’t wait to be able to do some strength training and focus on my triathlon training for july.

On to the run; I enjoyed it, but I’ve noticed my right knee has been sore since the weekends long run, so I made sure I did a warm up and included stretches. So I jogged round and dropped off the wifes Avon order, stretched and to liven up the run I tried a bit of fartlek between lamposts - really broke up the run and made it more enjoyable!

Mind you streching can cause injury, when back home my foot got attacked by the cat!

Day 4 - a Quick Blast

Jan 4th, 2011

Firstly, four days in a row - wow! I’m quite proud but I shan’t prattle on about it as it would get quite tiresome - so maybe just once a week!

Anyway, after a long run yesterday, I was a bit stiff and found my knee and hip was sore afterwards. After a day of popping into London with my daughter and walking round the Science Museum (the interactive bits are excellent!) the stiffness in my hip is nearly gone. I think it wise for me to include rest days during janathon otherwise I won’t last the distance!

So I planned a run round the estate and I felt great! Surprisingly, I managed my best pace for over 10 years! (Not that I overly did much running in the last 10 :p ). Hip felt ok and I made sure I stretched it out well afterwards. Depending on how it is tomorrow, I may do a longer run tomorrow…
