Day 13 - One Wrong Way Down a One Way

Jan 13th, 2011

Boris Bikes rule!  I got to Kings X but alas my first experience at 7:30am was an empty dock, so a quick jaunter over to the nearest dock and vola! I was in charge of a bike.   The bike is sturdy to say the least, but I reckon it was better than the last bike I owned, so 12 minutes and one wrong way down a one way later, I had reached my work - simples!  Next time I’ll have a better route.

The only downside today was on leaving the office tonight it was chucking it down, so I opted for the crush of the tube and I feel guilty about it - go figure?!

Day 10 - Walking to Recover

Jan 10th, 2011

Back to work today after a long christmas break and as mentioned yesterday I decided to take a day off running today to let the knees rest and to help recover.  So I side stepped the crush of the tube and walked from Kings Cross to Soho and it took under 30 mins.  I took a better route back and it took under 25 minutes, probably because the local crackheads seemed to be out in force and shouting randomly at passers by. Perversely, my knee is now aching more than ever…

Good news about my Boris Bike key, the registration has finally been confirmed and the key should arrive soon and then that will be my daily commute.

Day 9 - Batteries Running Low

Jan 9th, 2011

Cold and windy this morning! Knee still a little sore on the run, so had a cold bath when I got back - ouch, that’ll take some getting used to!  The good news is my knee and legs feel better after it.

The run itself; I felt out of energy on the hills as janathon is taking its toll, I dont have the base level of fitness so I am putting the body under some stress and I’m not able to recharge between sessions.  To counter this if my Boris Bike key comes in time I’ll be able to bike to work, otherwise I’ll walk rather than tube and count that as my Janathon exercise.  I need to recover some energy as its the grim challenge next weekend!
